# About

## Partners

The 'Train-the-Trainers Research Data Management Bootcamp' was co-organised by
the Social Sciences and Humanities Open Cloud
([SSHOC](https://sshopencloud.eu/)) project, and the
[DARIAH Research Data Management Working Group](https://www.dariah.eu/activities/working-groups/research-data-management/).

The SSHOC project, "Social Sciences and Humanities Open Cloud", has received
funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 project call
H2020-INFRAEOSC-04-2018, grant agreement #823782
